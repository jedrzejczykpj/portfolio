About

Me

Hi,
My name is Piotr and I am a Web Designer based in London.
I focus on designing and optimizing user interfaces.

Programming was always a huge part of my life. I fell in love with it as a child, watching my older cousins playing with TV wired Commodore.
My first PC in the '90s was a stepping stone to my dreams of coding and adventures of computer technologies. We didn't have any Internet access in those days, so magazines and books were my main source of information.

When I arrived in London, the doors of opportunity opened and the world of knowledge beckoned.
I wanted to build programming tools and websites, so I enrolled myself on a self-funding course at Just IT company.

Today, I use my creativity and skills to realise those coding dreams of mine.
